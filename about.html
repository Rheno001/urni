Unveiling & Rebranding Nigeria Initiative

We project the positive image of Nigeria through technology and community engagement.

Who We Are

Unveiling and Rebranding Nigeria Initiative is a nonprofit organization committed to showcasing Nigeria’s true potential; its people, heritage, and promise. Using technology and media, we present a vibrant and dynamic Nigeria to the world. Our efforts focus on two key areas: technology and community engagement

The Technology section harnesses the power of information technology to provide information with verifiable facts & figures about Nigeria. The Community engagement program deals with the non-technology aspects such as coordination and promotion of events that galvanize nation-building interactions such as town hall meetings, entrepreneurial skill trainings, opinion polls, tourist attraction Tour, etc.

Worthy of mention is that this initiative is promoted by a team of active citizens from different walks of life and championed by some distinguished Nigerians among whom is Ambassador Hassan Ardo Tukur, MFR, OFR, who has served the country in different capacities over the last four (4) decades.

Our Mission

To protect the positive image of Nigeria; using technology and community engagement to inspire patriotism in Nigerians.

Our Vision

To foster a nation where Nigerians, both home and abroad, celebrate their heritage and actively contribute to the country's development.

Our Core Values

  • Patriotism
  • Unity
  • Empathy

Our Target Audience

  • Nigerians as individuals
  • The International Community

Our Patron

Amb. Hassan Ardo Tukur, MFR, OFR Patron, Unveiling and Rebranding Nigeria Initiative

Ambassador Hassan Ardo Tukur is a visionary Nigerian diplomat whose career is marked by a profound dedication to peace, development, and global cooperation.

Serving as a Senior Special Assistant to the President on International Relations, Ambassador Hassan Ardo Tukur has been a pivotal figure in strengthening Nigeria’s diplomatic ties and advocating for unity across Africa. His leadership and strategic insights have not only elevated Nigeria's international standing but have also inspired future leaders to pursue transformative change. His legacy is one of hope, resilience, and unwavering commitment to a better world.
