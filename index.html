Interesting Facts & Figures About Nigeria

Nigeria is a land of rich culture, rapid growth, and remarkable achievements. Explore these fascinating facts and figures that make Nigeria truly unique.

Independence

1st of October, 1960

Nigeria gained her independence from Britain on the 1st of October, 1960.

Ethnic Groups

250+

Nigeria has over 250 ethnic groups, including the Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo, each contributing to the country’s rich cultural identity and heritage.

Population

230 Million

Nigeria has a population of over 230 Million, making her the most populous country in Africa, and 4th most populous in the world.

Size

923,768 square km

This makes Nigeria the 10th largest country in Africa by landmass.

Languages

500+

Nigeria is home to over 500 languages, making her one of the most linguistically diverse countries in the world, reflecting its rich culture and heritage.
